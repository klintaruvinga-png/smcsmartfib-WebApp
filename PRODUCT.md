# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Retail traders seeking to level up to institutional‑grade capabilities, including new users with limited knowledge who need a comfortable, user‑friendly UI that can grow with them.

## Product Purpose
Enable traders to monitor positions, execute trades, and analyze market data—all in one dashboard that merges the best aspects of MetaTrader, TradingView, and a gamification engine.

## Brand Personality
smart, sleek, clean

## Anti‑references
Avoid overly colorful or cluttered interfaces; aim for a simple green‑white‑red light theme with a complementary dark theme.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity** – information is clear and easy to understand.
- **Trust** – design conveys reliability and security.
- **Efficiency** – enables quick actions and streamlined workflows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance.
